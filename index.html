Buy a Date with Shelby

Before my college roommate/best friend went to the Rockies to lead an environmental crew, she made me an adorable photo album of our trips together. I wanted to make something equally sentimental for her return. This is my "dating website" where you can buy dates with her. It is full of inside jokes and if you turn up your volume, you can hear her singing in the background. Her reaction was initially shock and anger, but soon became endearing and really happy. She is still available by the way.
